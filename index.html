About the Park

Nestled in the Terai region of Uttar Pradesh, Dudhwa National Park is a haven for nature lovers. Home to tigers, elephants, swamp deer, and a vibrant variety of bird species, this forest gem is an ecological paradise that blends rich biodiversity with scenic tranquility.
